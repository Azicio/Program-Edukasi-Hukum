# ⚖️ Program Edukasi Hukum Terstruktur
**Status:** Public Standalone | **Role:** Legal Literacy & Education

An intuitive, mobile-optimized dashboard designed to simplify complex legal concepts into digestible modules.

### 🏛️ Learning Modules
1. **Kamus Istilah:** Nested definitions of fundamental legal terms.
2. **Penjelasan Singkat:** Bulleted breakdowns of the "Due Process of Law" and legal status.
3. **Mengapa Penting:** Contextualizing legal rights for social balance.
4. **Referensi Hukum:** Direct citations from UUD 1945 and KUHAP.

### 🎨 Visual Standards
- **Theme:** Charcoal & Slate Gray (Reduced eye strain)
- **Highlights:** Deep Orange (Instructional focus)
- **Typography:** Georgia Serif (Formal/Legal aesthetic)
